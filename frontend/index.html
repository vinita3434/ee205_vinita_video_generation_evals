How the judgement layer works

After you generate two videos, the judge runs them through three independent AI evaluators (Gemini 1.5 Pro, GPT-4o, Claude 3.5 Sonnet) to score quality on a 1–10 scale across: Identity Preservation, Scene Coherence, Temporal Fluency, Semantic Alignment, and Cinematic Quality.

Scores are combined into an overall quality score per model. This gives you comparable, repeatable metrics instead of guessing which output looks better.
